Climate change has affect our personal health!

Share

This self-diagnostic tool helps people examine their skin and take early protection and treatment.

There are several studies showing that solar radiation is the main cause of skin cancer such as melanoma. The most vulnerable group are descendants of white caucasians and their descendants, especially people living in high attitude with fair skin.

The core of this app was built on one of a top 1% of the first Healthcare Challenges by a leading french dermatology lab using technology from Microsoft and Google. The idea was developed during a hackathon in Martigny, September 2019.

Ref. https://www.who.int/globalchange/climate/summary/en/index5.html
